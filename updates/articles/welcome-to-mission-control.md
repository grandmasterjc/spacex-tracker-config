# Welcome to Mission Control

Welcome aboard. You've just joined thousands of space enthusiasts tracking every SpaceX launch in real time.

This app is your personal mission control — live countdowns, telemetry, webcasts, and launch odds, all in one place. Whether you're watching a Starlink mission go up at 3 AM or following a Starship test flight, we'll keep you informed from propellant load through orbital insertion.

## What you get for free

- **Launch countdown** to every upcoming SpaceX mission
- **Live status** and hold alerts
- **Mission timeline** with key events
- **Basic launch information** — vehicle, payload, launch site
- **24-hour launch reminders**
