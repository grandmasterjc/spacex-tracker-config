# Your Mission Control Premium Guide

Thanks for going premium. Here's everything that's now unlocked — and how to get the most out of it.

## Live Telemetry

When a launch goes live, tap it from the Dashboard to open the full flight view. You'll see:

- **MET counter** anchored to actual liftoff time
- **Altitude and velocity graph** drawn in real time against the expected flight profile
- **Current position marker** that matches exactly where the vehicle is on the curve
- **Live milestone timeline** — each event highlights as it passes

Open the app mid-flight? No problem. Everything catches up to the current mission elapsed time immediately.

## Integrated Webcasts

All SpaceX and third-party webcasts play inline — no more bouncing out to YouTube or X. Tap any stream card in the WEBCAST section to expand and watch.

## Pad Weather + Launch Odds

Under LAUNCH ODDS on each pre-launch detail view, you'll find live weather from the launch pad:

- Wind speed + gusts
- Temperature
- Cloud cover
- Visibility
- Precipitation (when present)

Weather refreshes every 5 minutes while the view is open. Data comes from Open-Meteo's global forecast API.

## Flight Replays

Every past mission has its YouTube replay linked in the history view. Tap Flight History → any mission → watch the full recording inline.

## Advanced Notifications

You now get notified at:

- **T-24 hours** — "Tomorrow's launch" summary
- **T-1 hour** — Go / No-go status
- **T-10 minutes** — Final countdown window
- **T-1 minute** — Liftoff imminent
- **Schedule changes** — Anytime NET shifts by more than 15 minutes

You can fine-tune which of these fire in Settings → Notifications.

## Mission Control Content

This feed will populate with exclusive deep dives: booster reuse analysis, Starship flight recaps, historical mission retrospectives, and rare archival footage. New articles drop every launch week.

## Tips

1. **Swipe left on the Dashboard hero card** to cycle to the next upcoming launch
2. **Pull down to refresh** the schedule at any time
3. **Long-press a launch card** to jump straight to the webcast when one is available

Questions? Settings → Contact us. We'll answer within 24 hours.

Thanks for supporting the app. Clear skies. 🚀
